Materials Needed:

1. Fresh Laundry - No one wants to fold dirty clothes!

2. Patience - We are going to be here a while...

Instructions:





Start with the shirt laid on a flat surface and spread out any wrinkles.











Fold both sleeves into the body of the shirt so that they are touhing.











Fold the bottom half of the shirt hamburger style to create a square shape.











Fold in the same manner as the previous step to create a rectangle shape.
