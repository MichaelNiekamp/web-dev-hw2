Nike

  • Nike was founded in 1964 as Blue Ribbon Sports by Bill Bowerman and Phil Knight. It was later renamed Nike, inspired by the Greek goddess of victory.

  • The iconic Nike "Swoosh" logo was designed by Carolyn Davidson in 1971. She was paid just $35 for her work, but the logo has become one of the most recognizable symbols worldwide.
  • Nike has consistently demonstrated strong financial performance. As of 2021, the company's annual revenue exceeded $44 billion, showcasing its enduring popularity and market dominance.

  • Nike operates in over 170 countries, making it one of the most internationally recognized brands.
