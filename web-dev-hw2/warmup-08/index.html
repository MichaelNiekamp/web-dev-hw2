Site Sketch


This website includes collapsable navigation bar from the right-hand side with social media links at the bottom that become highlighted when hovered over.

The site title, mission statement, and logo are fixed at the top of the screen so that as a user scrolls, those elements are always at the top.

3 photos are spaced equally apart with a description that appears when hovered over. The photos also act as links to different pages of the website.

A site description is positioned under the photos that gives the user information abouot the site.

Finally, the contact link at the bottom of the page will initiate an email to myself when clicked and changes color when hovered over.
